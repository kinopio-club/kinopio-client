# Affiliate Program

If you're a genuine user and fan of Kinopio, and you happen to an established (or semi-established) Youtube or TikTok creator, or you write for a publication, you may be eligible to join the Kinopio Affiliate Program.

Affiliates get a personalized referral promo URL that they can use to provide their audience with a 10% discount and earn 20% commissions.

For more details and how to sign up see the [Affiliate Program space](https://kinopio.club/kinopio-affiliate-program-YNmS6C3fofN3R9mYgO1Bu)

## Affiliate Dashboard

Once you're an affiliate you can use the [dashboard](https://kinopio.club/affiliate) to verify your promo URL, and view your pending payouts and commissions.

For integrating with other tracking systems, you can also fetch your commissions data through the public [API](https://kinopio.club/api).
