# @User Mentions and Due Dates

## @User

Type `@` in a card to assign cards to space collaborators, group members, as well as non-member contributors in an [Open to Comments](https://kinopio.club/help/posts/posts/sharing-spaces/) space. @mentioned users will be receive an email notification about the card.

<video autoplay loop muted playsinline class="">
  <source src="@/assets/pages/help/posts/mentions/add-user-mentions.mp4">
</video>

You can also bulk assign by paint selecting cards and using the `@` button

<video autoplay loop muted playsinline class="small">
  <source src="@/assets/pages/help/posts/mentions/paint-select-user-mention.mp4">
</video>

Use `Sidebar → @` to stay on top of the cards that have been assigned to you, in the current space or across all spaces.

<img src="@/assets/pages/help/posts/mentions/sidebar-at.webp" class="small">

-----
## Due Dates

You can also use `@` to add due dates to cards. Either by clicking a date, or by typing out a date with the following syntaxes:

- `@1d` will add a date `1` `d`ay from now. `@2d` will do `2` `d`ays from now. And so on.
- `@today` and `@tomorrow`
- `@nov20` or `@jan5` specify dates with the 3 letter month code and the day number (no spaces)

<video autoplay loop muted playsinline class="">
  <source src="@/assets/pages/help/posts/mentions/add-due-dates.mp4">
</video>

When the due date arrives, space members and group members who have contributed to the space will receive an email notification at 7am in their local timezone. Timezone is auto-detected but you can change it in `Settings → Date and Time`.

<img src="@/assets/pages/help/posts/mentions/timezone.webp" class="small">

Just like with Tasks, use `Sidebar → Due Dates` to view, and jump to, your upcoming due dates across all your spaces.

<img src="@/assets/pages/help/posts/mentions/sidebar-due.webp" class="small">

Bring it all together by combining [checkboxes](/help/checkbox-cards/) and [tags](/help/tags/) with @user mentions and due dates and you have all the elements of project management.

<img src="@/assets/pages/help/posts/mentions/combined.webp" class="">
