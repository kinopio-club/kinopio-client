# Getting Started

Kinopio is a spatial thinking tool for visually collecting and connecting your thoughts, ideas, and feelings.

## Cards

Click anywhere on a space to create a card, then type to fill it in. Drag cards around to arrange them.

## Connections

Drag from the connector dot on the edge of a card to another card to connect them.

## Spaces

Spaces are the canvases that hold your cards. Create a new space from the spaces list in the top left.
